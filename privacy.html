Privacy Policy

Shelfinder was built with privacy at the core. Here’s how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy explains how Shelfinder handles your information.

1. Data Storage & Security

All app settings, license info and UI state are stored locally on your Mac. Shelfinder does not upload your files or clipboard contents to any server.

2. No Analytics or Tracking

Shelfinder does not collect personal data, usage analytics or device identifiers.

3. License Activation

License validation happens locally. Your email/license key are stored only on your device for activation and never transmitted externally.

4. Payment Processing

Purchases are processed by Paddle. We do not store your payment details.

5. Your Rights

Because Shelfinder doesn’t collect external data, you retain full control on your device.

6. Contact

Questions? hello@shelfinder.app
