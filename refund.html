Refund Policy

Simple and fair. Here’s how refunds work with Shelfinder.

1. Try Before You Buy

Shelfinder offers a free version to help you evaluate the app before purchasing a license.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are handled by Paddle and evaluated case-by-case, typically within 14 days of purchase unless your local law requires otherwise.

3. When Refunds May Be Refused

Paddle may refuse a refund in cases of fraud, refund abuse or suspicious behavior. Requests after 14 days may be declined unless required by law.

4. Sales Tax Refunds

If charged sales tax and you’re eligible for exemption, you may request a tax refund within 60 days of purchase (proof required).

5. Technical Issues

If technical issues prevent proper use, contact us. We’ll help resolve or assist with a refund within the eligible timeframe.

6. Requesting a Refund

Email hello@shelfinder.app with your purchase email and order ID.
